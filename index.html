SporTrax – Tennis Score and Stats Tracker

SporTrax helps tennis players keep score on Apple Watch and review their performance on iPhone. Track points, games, sets, and match progress in real time — no phone needed during play.

🎾 Features

  • Apple Watch tennis scoring for points, games, sets, and matches
  • Quick undo for correcting the last point
  • Serve indicator support
  • Match history on iPhone
  • Detailed tennis match analytics
  • Practice session tracking and summaries
  • Heart rate, calories, and timer views when enabled on Apple Watch
  • Simple, focused design for use during play

⌚ Apple Watch App

Start a match from your wrist and stay focused without needing your phone during play. SporTrax includes quick controls for scoring, undoing the last point, tracking the server, and reviewing match progress.

📊 Match Analytics

Get a detailed breakdown of your performance directly on your wrist. SporTrax tracks advanced statistics throughout your match to help you identify strengths and weaknesses:

  • Point Breakdown: Total points played and won, including win percentages
  • Break Point Efficiency: Track how many break points you created and converted
  • Service Stats: Monitor your service points won and overall service win percentage
  • Receiving Stats: See how well you perform when returning

🏃 Practice Mode

SporTrax also supports practice tracking, so you can log practice activity and review practice summaries alongside your match history.

📱 iPhone Companion App

After the match, the iPhone companion app gives you a clear view of your tennis history and performance. Review match summaries, set-by-set details, timing, break point performance, service stats, receiving stats, and overall win percentages.

📈 Performance Summary

Aggregates every match you've played to show lifetime win percentages across key metrics — match & set win rates, break points, service, and receiving.

📋 Match History

The Activity log stores every match and practice session chronologically, showing who you played, when, and the final scores.

🔍 Match Detail

Dive deep into any match — box score, timing, and granular stats comparing your performance against your opponent.

🏃 Practice Summary

Review your practice sessions with the same level of detail as your matches, including health and activity data.

📝 Practice Detail

Get a full breakdown of each practice session, including duration, calories, and heart rate data.
