Mini games for training days

Play Tug of War without serving.

Tug of War is a fast Apple Watch mini game for rallies, drills, and competitive hitting sessions. It is great when you want points to matter but do not want the session to revolve around serving.

  • Start at 5. The shared score begins in the middle, with player 1 pulling toward 10 and player 2 pulling toward 0.
  • Let the rally develop. The ball has to go over the net twice before a point can score.
  • Move the score. Player 1 winning a point increments the score by 1. Player 2 winning a point decrements it by 1.
  • Win by pulling all the way. Player 1 wins at 10. Player 2 wins at 0.
