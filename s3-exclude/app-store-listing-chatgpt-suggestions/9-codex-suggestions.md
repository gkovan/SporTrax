Full description:
```text
Track your matches and training from Apple Watch, then review everything on iPhone.

SporTrax helps you score live sessions and analyze performance across Tennis, Pickleball, Tug Of War, and Practice. Capture activity while you play, then come back to detailed stats, heart rate data, and session history afterward.

FEATURES

Apple Watch live scoring
Track points, games, sets, rallies, and match progress directly from your wrist so you can stay focused during play.

Multi-sport support
Use SporTrax for Tennis, Pickleball, Tug Of War, and Practice sessions in one app.

Detailed match analytics
Review saved activity with box scores, game and set breakdowns, point and rally stats, timing, and win percentages.

Heart rate and calorie tracking
Capture heart rate and active calories during supported sessions and review average heart rate, max heart rate, heart rate zones, and time in each zone.

Heart rate zone analytics
See how your effort changes across Tennis, Pickleball, Tug Of War, and Practice with post-session heart rate zone summaries.

Practice tracking
Log practice sessions and review duration, calories, heart rate, and saved sample history.

Manual activity entry
Add Tennis, Pickleball, Tug Of War, and Practice activities manually so your history stays complete.

Career summary view
See cumulative totals for time played, calories burned, matches, games, points, rallies, and practice sessions.

Shareable activity views
Share a snapshot of your activity detail view with others using Messages, Mail, WhatsApp, and more.

WHY SPORTRAX

SporTrax is built for players who want more than a final score. It combines live Apple Watch tracking with post-session analysis so you can understand both performance and effort over time.

Whether you are playing a competitive tennis match, a pickleball game, a tug of war session, or just getting reps in during practice, SporTrax helps you track it, review it, and improve.
```
