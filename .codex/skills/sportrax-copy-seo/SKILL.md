# SporTrax Copy And SEO

Use this skill for copy, metadata, and positioning work in `/Users/gerrykovan/Documents/github/SporTrax`.

## Product Brief

SporTrax is an Apple Watch-first sports tracking app. Current messaging should emphasize:

- Tennis scoring from Apple Watch.
- Match stats, history, summaries, and analytics.
- Practice tracking and activity history.
- Health, calories, and heart-rate context where supported.
- iPhone views that summarize and review Apple Watch activity.

## Voice

- Clear, polished, sporty, and practical.
- Product-specific rather than generic fitness-app language.
- Confident without overclaiming precision, outcomes, or medical/health benefits.
- Short headings; useful supporting copy.
- Prefer concrete feature language over hype.

## SEO Guidance

- Keep title and meta description aligned with Apple Watch tennis scoring and sports tracking.
- Preserve brand visibility: SporTrax should appear in primary title and important headings.
- Include likely search language naturally: Apple Watch tennis score tracker, tennis stats, match history, practice tracking.
- Avoid keyword stuffing.

## Privacy And Claims

- Do not invent data practices, integrations, subscriptions, or health capabilities.
- Treat health, heart-rate, and calorie language carefully; present as tracking/context, not diagnosis or guaranteed accuracy.
- For privacy wording, keep it plain and conservative.

## Workflow

1. Read the target copy in context before editing.
2. Keep the strongest product promise visible early.
3. Preserve factual claims unless repo evidence supports changing them.
4. After editing, scan for repeated phrases, vague claims, and mobile-unfriendly long headings.
