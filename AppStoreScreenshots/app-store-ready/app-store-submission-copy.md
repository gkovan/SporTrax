## Promotional Text

Track tennis scores on Apple Watch, then review match history, practice sessions, and performance summaries on iPhone.

## Description

SporTrax helps tennis players keep score on Apple Watch and review their performance on iPhone.

Start a match from your wrist, track points, games, sets, and match progress in real time, and stay focused without needing your phone during play. SporTrax includes quick controls for match scoring, undoing the last point, tracking the server, and reviewing match progress.

After the match, the iPhone companion app gives you a clear view of your tennis history and performance. Review match summaries, set-by-set details, timing, break point performance, service stats, receiving stats, and overall win percentages.

SporTrax also supports practice tracking, so you can log practice activity and review practice summaries alongside your match history.

Features:

- Apple Watch tennis scoring for points, games, sets, and matches
- Quick undo for correcting the last point
- Serve indicator support
- Match history on iPhone
- Detailed tennis match analytics
- Practice session tracking and summaries
- Heart rate, calories, and timer views when enabled on Apple Watch
- Simple, focused design for use during play

Whether you are playing casually, practicing, or tracking competitive matches, SporTrax keeps your score and performance history close at hand.
